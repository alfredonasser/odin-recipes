Easy Hummus (Better Than Store-Bought)

Perfectly flavored hummus, jordan style

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (or garbanzo beans)
  • Tahini
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Fresh garlic
  • Ground cumin and salt
  • Olive oil
  • Roasted Red Peppers

Steps

  1. Tahini and lemon juice go in first.
  2. Then before you add anything else, turn on the food processor and let it run for a minute or so.
  3. From here, you can add ingredients as you will, but make sure you stand back and let that food processor run — we’re talking a minute of running after adding each ingredient.
  4. Cold water (or aquafaba — explained below) is our last trick for fluffy, creamy, whipped hummus.
